pingpot GitHub

A coffee-pot status web app, originally proposed as a joke, now made real. Allows coffee pot fill level tracking, as well as pot temperature and coffee supply contribution lists. Meant to be paired with the postpot coffeepot sensor.

Favorite Feature: Muggy

A humorous talking mug mascot was included because the layout was very condensed and used little screen real estate.

Muggy cycles through several coffee-based sayings to keep spirits high.

Muggy was one of the first interesting things I made using SVG editors. His flat design and basic and exaggerated features made him an excellent beginner's study.

The mug mascot was borrowed from a relatively obscure video game character from the Old World Blues DLC for Fallout: New Vegas.
