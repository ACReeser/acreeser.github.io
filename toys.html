Time Slice was a tiny project that I did to learn HTML5/Javascript/CSS, and the JQuery and JQuery UI libraries. The game has a 'snapshot' mechanic. The player captures a specific moment in time and tries to match combos like doubles, triples, or palindromes.

PLAY

Controls:

  • Space: Take a slice of time
  • S: Slow down time
