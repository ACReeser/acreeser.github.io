Warped Drive Github

In Warped Drive, the player is a lone IT engineer of a behemoth colony ship headed for the far reaches of the galaxy. Unfortunately for you and the colony, your ship’s computer has caught a nasty virus, and it’s wreaking havoc with your systems. You can only wake from hyper-sleep for so long before you run out of oxygen, so you better use your time and resources wisely.

Warped Drive is a mix of 2D survival and 3D strategy. In 2D meatspace, oxygen is scarce and doors are invariably locked. In 3D cyberspace, the player must send waves of custom antivirus ships to do battle with nasty viruses.

Tech: Unity
